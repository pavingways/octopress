--- layout: post title: O2 and Vodafone launch mpass date: '2008-10-20' categories: - News tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' comments: true --- O2 and Vodafone have launched their new payment service "mpass" in Germany. Through mpass mobile phone customers with any German provider now have access to secure online shopping on their mobile phone or PC.

mpass combines the direct debiting system (German: Lastschriftverfahren) with SMS payment confirmation through mobile phones (we blogged about this before). Some companies like Cinemaxx, Blume2000.de or Beate Uhse are already supporting the service.

More about mpass here: http://www.mpass.de/
