--- layout: post title: Books I recommend date: '2007-08-24' categories: - Others tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} comments: true --- The last couple of days we spent some time at Borders, a great bookstore. You have a really broad selection of books there and a cozy coffee shop inside, where you can read or do your work. I also love Amazon. So, the following list contains books from both retailers :)

My list of great books that in my opinion really help starting your (web) business:
  • Guy Kawasaki: The Art of the Start
  • Guy Kawasaki: How to Drive Your Competition Crazy
  • Malcolm Gladwell: The Tipping Point
  • James Surowiecki: The Wisdom of Crowds
  • Mark Hughes: Buzzmarketing
  • Jessica Livingston: Founders at Work
  • John L. Nesheim: High Tech Start Up
  • Chris Anderson: The Long Tail
  • Keith Sawyer: Group Genius
  • Chris Murray: The Marketing Gurus

If you have read or know another must-read book, let me know!
